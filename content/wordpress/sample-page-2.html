Refine tomorrow.** If you are waiting for the perfect moment to start or the perfect product to start with, my bets are that you will keep on waiting for ever. Quality is important when there is enough of quantity. If you have not yet started doing something, start doing it and then think about making it better. Think about the quality of your articles only after you start writing. Before that your concern should be about starting. Think about scaling your web application after you get enough traffic. Before that think of ways to get good traffic. So I rebuild this site using wordpress.
